Shlokas for New Beginnings

Every new beginning in Hindu tradition — whether a business venture, a new home, a wedding, or a new year — is consecrated with sacred shlokas and prayers. Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles, is invoked first. These shlokas bring divine blessings to any fresh start in life.

01
वक्रतुण्ड महाकाय सूर्यकोटिसमप्रभ। निर्विघ्नं कुरु मे देव सर्वकार्येषु सर्वदा॥
Vakratunda mahakaya suryakoti samaprabha, nirvighnam kuru me deva sarvakaryeshu sarvada
✦
O Lord Ganesha of curved trunk and mighty body, brilliant as a million suns — make all my endeavors free from obstacles, always.
Ganesha Prarthana
02
ॐ श्री गणेशाय नमः।
Om Shri Ganeshaya Namah
✦
Salutations to Lord Ganesha. This simple, powerful mantra is chanted before every new beginning, every auspicious occasion, every important work.
Ganesha Mantra
03
सह नाववतु। सह नौ भुनक्तु। सह वीर्यं करवावहै।
Saha navavatu, saha nau bhunaktu, saha viryam karavavahai
✦
May we be protected together. May we be nourished together. May we work with great energy together. A beautiful prayer for any new shared journey.
Taittiriya Upanishad 2.2
04
ॐ असतो मा सद्गमय। तमसो मा ज्योतिर्गमय।
Om asato ma sadgamaya, tamaso ma jyotirgamaya
✦
Lead me from untruth to truth, from darkness to light. Every new beginning is an opportunity to move toward the light.
Brihadaranyaka Upanishad 1.3.28
05
सर्वे भवन्तु सुखिनः।
Sarve bhavantu sukhinah
✦
May all beings be happy. Begin every new chapter of your life by extending your goodwill to all of creation.
Brihadaranyaka Upanishad
