Shlokas About Surrender

Surrender (Sharanagati) is the ultimate act of faith in Hindu spirituality — the moment when the ego dissolves and the soul rests entirely in the divine. It is not weakness but the highest strength, the courage to let go of control and trust in a power greater than oneself. These shlokas guide the seeker toward total surrender.

01
सर्वधर्मान्परित्यज्य मामेकं शरणं व्रज।
Sarva dharman parityajya mam ekam saranam vraja
✦
Abandon all varieties of dharma and surrender unto Me alone. I shall deliver you from all sins — do not grieve.
Bhagavad Gita 18.66
02
तमेव शरणं गच्छ सर्वभावेन भारत। तत्प्रसादात्परां शान्तिं स्थानं प्राप्स्यसि शाश्वतम्॥
Tam eva sharanam gaccha sarvabhavena bharata, tatprasadat param shantim sthanam prapsyasi shashvatam
✦
Take refuge in Him alone with all your being. By His grace you will attain the supreme peace and the eternal abode.
Bhagavad Gita 18.62
03
अनन्यचेताः सततं यो मां स्मरति नित्यशः।
Ananyachetah satatam yo mam smarati nityashah
✦
For one who remembers Me constantly with an undivided mind, I am easy to reach. Surrender is remembrance.
Bhagavad Gita 8.14
04
कायेन वाचा मनसेन्द्रियैर्वा बुद्ध्यात्मना वा प्रकृतेः स्वभावात्। करोमि यद्यत्सकलं परस्मै नारायणायेति समर्पयामि॥
Kayena vacha manasendriyairva buddhyatmana va prakriteh svabhavat, karomi yadyat sakalam parasmai narayanayeti samarpayami
✦
Whatever I do with my body, speech, mind, or senses — I offer it all to Lord Narayana. This is the essence of total surrender.
Mukunda Mala
05
त्वमेव माता च पिता त्वमेव। त्वमेव बन्धुश्च सखा त्वमेव। त्वमेव विद्या द्रविणं त्वमेव। त्वमेव सर्वं मम देव देव॥
Tvameva mata cha pita tvameva, tvameva bandhush cha sakha tvameva, tvameva vidya dravinam tvameva, tvameva sarvam mama deva deva
✦
You alone are my mother, father, brother, and friend. You alone are my knowledge and my wealth. You are everything to me, O Lord of Lords.
Pandava Gita
