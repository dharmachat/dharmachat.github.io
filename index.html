FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is DharmaChat?+

DharmaChat is the world's most comprehensive Hindu scripture app, helping devotees explore the Bhagavad Gita, Mahabharata, Ramayana, Upanishads, Vedas, and Puranas through AI-powered conversation, shloka discovery, character studies, and daily dharmic guidance.

Is DharmaChat free?+

Yes! DharmaChat is free for everyone. 10 free questions every day with no credit card required. A Premium Plan is available for those who wish to support our mission and unlock unlimited questions.

How do I know the answers are scripturally accurate?+

Our AI is trained exclusively on authentic Hindu scriptures and refined with guidance from qualified pandits, acharyas, and Vedic scholars. Every answer is grounded in actual scriptural references, and we continuously improve our model through scholarly review.

Which scriptures does DharmaChat cover?+

DharmaChat covers 18+ sacred texts including the Bhagavad Gita, Mahabharata (all 18 Parvas), Ramayana (Valmiki & Tulsi), the 108 Upanishads, four Vedas, 18 Mahapuranas, Yoga Sutras, Arthashastra, Manusmriti, and more.

Is DharmaChat suitable for all Hindu traditions?+

Absolutely. DharmaChat welcomes devotees from all sampradayas — Vaishnavism, Shaivism, Shaktism, Smartism, and others. The experience adapts to your preferred tradition and deity focus.

Who created DharmaChat?+

DharmaChat was founded by Sukhil Babu from Bengaluru, India — an entrepreneur and devotee who believed that modern technology can deepen our connection to Sanatana Dharma. Our work is guided by learned pandits and Vedic scholars.
