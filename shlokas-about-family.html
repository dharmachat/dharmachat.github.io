Shlokas About Family

Family (Kutumba) is the foundation of Hindu dharma. From the joint family tradition to the deep reverence for parents and elders, Hindu scriptures teach that family is the first ashram — the place where dharma, love, and spiritual growth begin. These shlokas honor the bonds of family.

01
मातृदेवो भव। पितृदेवो भव। आचार्यदेवो भव।
Matru devo bhava. Pitru devo bhava. Acharya devo bhava.
✦
Treat your mother as God. Treat your father as God. Treat your teacher as God. The family is the first temple.
Taittiriya Upanishad 1.11
02
जननी जन्मभूमिश्च स्वर्गादपि गरीयसी।
Janani janmabhoomishcha swargadapi gariyasi
✦
Mother and motherland are greater than heaven itself. No bond is more sacred than the one with your mother.
Valmiki Ramayana 6.124.17
03
पितुर्दशगुणा माता गौरवेण।
Pitur dashaguna mata gauravena
✦
A mother's glory is ten times greater than a father's. Hindu scriptures place the mother at the highest pedestal of reverence within the family.
Manusmriti 2.145
04
कुलधर्मे नष्टे कुलं कृत्स्नमधर्मो भिभवत्युत।
Kuladharme nashte kulam kritsnam adharmo bhibhavatyuta
✦
When family dharma is destroyed, the whole family is overwhelmed by unrighteousness. Protecting family values is protecting dharma itself.
Bhagavad Gita 1.40
05
सर्वे भद्राणि पश्यन्तु मा कश्चिद्दुःखभाग्भवेत्।
Sarve bhadrani pashyantu ma kashchid duhkhabhag bhavet
✦
May all see goodness. May no one suffer. This universal family prayer extends the concept of family to include all of humanity.
Brihadaranyaka Upanishad
